About Us

We are five students who have built a system that will enable effective communication between the patients at home and the professional staff at the hospital, with an emphasis on health services provided remotely and immediately

Our goals:

  • Building a friendly website for a patient who lacks technological knowledge.
  • Increasing patient satisfaction with the service provided to them
  • Setting an appointment that takes into account the patient's data and calculates its urgency.
  • Responding to the patient, in an emergency situation or in need of medical information by a doctor without having to go to the emergency room.
